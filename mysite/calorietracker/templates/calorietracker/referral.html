CalorieTracker.io Referral Program

Our goal is to build the best weight and fitness assistant possible and help CalorieTracker.io users share their journey and experiences with others. As a user, partaking in the referral program will allow you to earn rewards every time your referral link is used by your friends and family when they register and start using CalorieTracker.io. Referrals rewards are accredited for referred usesers that sign up through your unique referral link. Note that a referral reward can not be deemed retroactively after a user has already signed up without your link.

You and anyone using your referral link can each earn 10 gold coins on CalorieTracker.io. Gold coins will be able to to be redeemed in the future for various features, membership status and rewards coming soon to CalorieTracker.io!
